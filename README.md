# Voice AI Demo

A browser-based demo that shows how AI voice agents handle phone calls. It simulates a conversation between a customer calling a business and an AI receptionist that picks up.

No real phone calls here — it's a visual walkthrough of what the experience looks and sounds like, with a chat-style transcript that plays out in real time.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Click "Start Call"
3. Watch the conversation play out between the caller and the AI agent

## What It Shows

- How an AI receptionist greets callers
- How it handles appointment booking
- How it asks the right questions and confirms details
- The natural back-and-forth flow of a voice AI conversation

## Why This Exists

Most people have never interacted with a voice AI agent. This demo gives them a quick feel for what it's like — without needing to actually ring a number.

We build real voice AI agents at [Hand On Web](https://www.handonweb.com). This is just a taster.

## Tech

HTML + CSS + vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools. Open the file and it works.
